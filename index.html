Welcome to Our Lab

My lab aims at using computational and theoretical methods to uncover materials' physical chemistry chemical physics for applications in energy management, mass transport, information processing, and human health improvement. My research interests include thermal transport, polymer science, interface engineering, nanotechnology, multi-scale simulation, machine learning, data science, and artificial intelligence. I will develop Physics-based computational models with High Performance Computers. Through rigorous calculation and interdisciplinary collaboration, my research will have a broad impact in thermal physics, theoretical and computational chemistry, materials science, and data science.
